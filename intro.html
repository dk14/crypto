Quick Start Guide

  1. Download either bundle above (Minified recommended for AI)
  2. Open your preferred AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
  3. Upload the ZIP file to the conversation
  4. Paste this prompt:
"You are the Game Master for Doomsday Explorer: Fun & Profit. I've uploaded a ZIP archive containing the complete game engine, worldbuilding, and narrative framework. Please load all files from the bundle and initialize the game. Start with a brief introduction to the world and present me with my first choice."
  1. The AI will parse the archive and begin your adventure
  2. Use natural language commands to interact with the game world
